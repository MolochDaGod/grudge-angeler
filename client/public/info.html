How To Play

From novice to master angler in 7 simple steps.

1
Choose Your Character
Pick from 3 unique fisherman variants: Classic, Ocean Blue, or Crimson. Enter your name to begin.
2
Walk & Explore
Use A/D keys to walk along the pier. Press E near the hut to open the shop, or E near the boat to board it.
3
Cast Your Line
Click to start casting. Move your mouse to aim the crosshair, then click again to release the bobber into the water.
4
Wait for a Bite
Watch your bobber and the fish swimming below. When a fish bites, you'll see an exclamation mark. Click fast!
5
Reel It In
A reeling minigame starts. Click to move the catch zone. Keep the fish icon inside the green zone to fill the progress gauge.
6
Sell & Upgrade
Fish are automatically sold for money. Visit the hut shop (press E) to buy better rods, bait, and lures.
7
Go Deep
Board the boat with E and sail into the open ocean. Rarer and larger fish spawn further from shore. Hunt the ultra-rares!

Game Features

A deep fishing experience packed into a pixel-perfect package.

Dynamic Market
Fish prices fluctuate based on supply and demand. Selling the same fish repeatedly drops its price. Diversify your catches for maximum profit!
Bounty Board
Three rotating bounties challenge you to catch specific fish at minimum sizes. Complete them all for big cash rewards and new bounties appear.
Boat Exploration
Board the boat and sail into the open ocean. The camera follows you across 3 screens of ocean. Deeper waters hold rarer, larger fish.
Fish Collection Log
Track every species you've caught with detailed stats: biggest size, total weight, catch count, and rarity badges. Complete the full collection!
Day/Night Cycle
Watch the sky shift through beautiful color gradients. Stars appear at night. The ambient atmosphere changes constantly.
Swimming & Diving
Jump off the pier and swim underwater! Explore the ocean depths, watch fish swim past, and climb back up when you're ready to fish again.
Combo System
Catch fish consecutively without missing to build combos. Higher combos mean bigger rewards. One miss resets the streak!
9 Ultra-Rare Creatures
Legendary variants of every fish species exist with glowing auras and massive sizes. They're incredibly rare, extremely difficult to reel in, and worth a fortune.
